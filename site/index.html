Vela is a free, open-source maps and navigation app for Android. Live place data, traffic-aware turn-by-turn, transit boards and real offline support. No account. No API key. No Play Services.

Download APK View source
GPLv3 · Android 10+ · works on GrapheneOS and every de-googled ROM
What you get

Everything the big app does. None of the strings.

The map data people actually rely on, pulled live and anonymously, drawn on open vector tiles.

📍

Live place data

Ratings, reviews, hours, photos, busy times and phone numbers for real businesses, fetched fresh when you look.

🧭

Turn-by-turn with traffic

Spoken guidance with lane diagrams, highway shields, live traffic ETAs, faster-route offers and smart rerouting.

🚌

Transit that works

Live departure boards, stop timelines and transit directions from open GTFS feeds plus traffic-aware estimates.

📶

Properly offline

Download whole states: map tiles, routing graphs, place search and a typed-address geocoder that all work with zero signal.

🗣️

Neural voices, on device

Natural-sounding guidance in 14 languages through downloadable voices that never touch a server.

📷

Knows about the cameras

Community-mapped ALPR cameras drawn on the map and counted per route, so you can pick the quiet way. Opt-in.

Privacy
Your map should not have a memory.
  • No account, no sign-in, no API key
  • No Play Services, no Firebase, no analytics
  • Your GPS trace never leaves the phone
  • Place lookups go out anonymously, from your device
  • Every network call is documented, and the optional ones have switches
